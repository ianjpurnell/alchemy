Basis in Science:

Alchemy mainly focussed on the turning of inexpensive materials into gold, which doesn’t make any kind of sense economically, but whatever. Nuclear transmutation is the changing of one element into another by changing the amount of protons in an atom. Since the number of protons is what makes an element what it is, it could be construed as alchemy, although it's not really using any traditional alchemical methods.
