Modern Alchemy:

The idea of changing things to other things is definitely still prevalent in science today. Transmutation and other aspects of atomic theory have been tested and proven many times over. However, the term alchemy is not one used to describe these processes. The word is now only usually used in reference to wizardry and mysticism, perhaps rightly so. However, there are still many modern alchemists today trying to prove that it is possible.
