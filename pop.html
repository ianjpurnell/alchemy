Pop Culture:

In pop culture: Alchemy makes many appearances in popular culture, including music, theatre, books, and movies. The philosopher’s stone, a legendary relic that supposedly can turn things to gold, is in the first Harry Potter book and movie, as well as numerous other novels. It is often portrayed as somewhat fantastical, and magic and mysticism are grouped with it in many places its mentioned. Nicholas flamel, a famous alchemist is also mentioned many times in media.
