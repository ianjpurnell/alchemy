Misconceptions:

There is often a stereotype associated with alchemy is a cultish kind of feeling with black robes and an obsession with the philosopher's stone. Contrary to popular belief, it is not just about turning lead into gold, but the idea of transmutaion, turning one kind of matter into another kind of matter. There is also a myth especially in europe that the church in europe persecuted them for their strange ways. While they did ban their monks from distilling and alchemy, alchemists themselves were not persecuted. Alchemy is also not at all associated with the illuminati, the Knights templar, or any other conspiracies. The way the media has portrayed alchemy and the philosopher's stone may have construed the truths about it.
